Sticky Notes for Your Website

Stickies! is designed to simplify client and team interaction on website projects in progress. Use Stickies! on your pre-launch website or staging environment.

Visual Collaboration

Stickies! enables visual collaboration between you and your team. Leave sticky notes on any page of your site, anywhere on the page.

Easy to Use

Click to create a new Stickies! note, drag it anywhere on the screen, and type your note. Visitors will see the note just where you left it.

Easy to Install

Sign up, copy and paste one line of JavaScript and upload one HTML file to your web server. That's it!

It‘s Free!

Stickies! is 100% free! Use it on as many sites as you like. We hope you enjoy it!
